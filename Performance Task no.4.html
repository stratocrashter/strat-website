My Favorite Food

By: Larenze Bandal

Dessert (Pancake)

Pancake (or hotcake, griddlecake, or flapjack) is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter, and then cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan.

Dish (Chicken Adobo)

Adobong manok is a traditional stew originating from the Philippines, this dish has a lot of types but this one was made with a combination of chicken, garlic, onions, bay leaves, peppercorns, oil, brown sugar, soy sauce, and white vinegar.
